About StegSecure

A next-generation multimedia steganography platform integrating AES encryption, SHA-256 authentication, and covert secure communication.

What is Steganography?

Steganography is the technique of concealing confidential information within ordinary digital media such as images, audio, and video files while hiding the very existence of the communication itself. Unlike encryption, which makes data unreadable but visible, steganography focuses on invisibility. In StegSecure, advanced Least Significant Bit (LSB) techniques are used to securely embed encrypted payloads with minimal perceptual distortion.

What is Cryptography?

Cryptography is the science of protecting sensitive information through encryption techniques that transform readable data into secure unreadable ciphertext. StegSecure utilizes AES encryption for strong confidentiality protection and SHA-256 hashing for secure password authentication, ensuring that only authorized users can access hidden information.

What is StegSecure?

StegSecure is a modern multimedia cybersecurity platform that combines cryptography and steganography to provide secure hidden communication across image, audio, and video formats. The system encrypts confidential information using AES encryption and securely conceals the encrypted payload using:

Image LSB

Audio LSB

Video EOF

AES + SHA-256

StegSecure combines advanced cryptography, multimedia steganography, and analytical performance evaluation into a unified cybersecurity platform for secure and covert digital communication.
